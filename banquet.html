Banquet Halls

Swayamvara & Swadishta

A long corridor embellished with beautiful glass art leads you to our 2 exclusive buffet halls - ‘Swayamvara’ & ‘Swadishta’, each with a seating capacity of 200 and 125 respectively. Aptly christened based on the fascinating Sanskrit words, ‘Swayamvara’ refers to the vast variety of cuisine offered, from which you can choose (as in, a ‘Swayamvar’ of food), while ‘Swadishta’ denotes the lip smacking, delectable spread, ready to titillate your palate. Magnificently intricate & antique paintings adorn the walls of both the halls, flaunting the long-forgotten Indian craftsmanship and reminding us of our rich cultural heritage.

Be it birthdays, anniversaries, baby showers, official conferences, or even a simple high tea; look no further than our buffet halls to host them! Aesthetically-pleasing soundproof partition separates the 2 halls, as we respect the privacy of our guests. Alternatively, if you’d like to have a single, large banquet hall, the partition could be removed to club the 2 halls, thus expanding the capacity to 400. “So when are you coming here to experience royalty?”
